# quote-wallpaper (명언 바탕화면)

Fullscreen book quote display that loops through 150+ hand-transcribed passages from ~20 years of reading. Quotes are categorized by theme (wisdom, growth, mindset, success, learning, productivity, relationship) and rotate every 5 minutes with a word-by-word fade-in animation. Clicking advances to the next quote manually.

**Logic:** Random selection from the pool, filtered to avoid consecutive quotes from the same book. Each quote carries a `category` tag and `book` attribution.

Also includes an iOS Scriptable widget (`scriptable-quotes-widget.js`) for home screen display.

Stack: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, Gowun Dodum font, Wikimedia background image.
